About

Welcome to ICGSD 2025 Conference

We would like to extend our warmest invitation to you for participating in 2025 International Conference on Green and Sustainable Development: Harmonizing Environmental and Economic Dimensions (ICGSD 2025) that will be held in Tashkent, Uzbekistan during February 17-19, 2025.

As countries around the world continue to integrate economically, many are restructuring their economies to harness the power of science and technological innovation for growth. The traditional model of extensive economic development has, over the past few decades, led to environmental damage, low efficiency, and high capital costs. This outdated approach no longer meets the needs of the modern era, highlighting the necessity for transformation and innovation. In response to this, ICGSD2025 focuses on economic development and the latest knowladge, offering theoretical foundations for sustainable and rapid growth.
